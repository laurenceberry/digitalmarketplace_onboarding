What you will need to do

  • Create a supplier account for your company and become a contributor.
  • Apply to sell your services when applications are open.
Read the Digital Marketplace suppliers' guide for more information.

Before you start

To create a supplier account, you’ll need your company’s:

  • head office DUNS number
  • Companies House number (if you have one)
  • contact details

When you have set up your account we will send you a confirmation email. You must click on the link in the email to activate your supplier account.
